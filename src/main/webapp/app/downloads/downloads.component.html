Application Form Downloads

To download application form(s) for any land transaction, kindly run through the form generation wizard to generate specific/personalized application form for your desired land transaction. Information required from the wizard include the transaction of interest, count of parties (individual or organisation); as well as if the form is for an agent on behalf of the actual applicant(s).

Note: If you have already have a portal account, you can also fill and submit your application form online. Click here to register portal account, have your account up and runninng in few minutes.
